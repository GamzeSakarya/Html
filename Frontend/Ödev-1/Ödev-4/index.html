Christmas Cookies Recipe

Ingeredients

  • 1 cup butter (at room temperature)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Colored sprinkles or granulated sugar for topping

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the egg and mix well. Then, add the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet mixture, stirring until a soft dough forms
  6. Take walnut-sized pieces of dough, roll them into balls, and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving some space between each.
  7. Sprinkle colored sprinkles or granulated sugar on top of the cookies.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden brown.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet before transferring them to a wire rack.

Enjoy your cookies, and have a happy Christmas!
